Traffix

Traffix is a more effective traffic gauging system that relieves traffic congestion by prioritizing lanes with the most automobiles. The project is a physical simulator in Arduino with a developed algorithm to efficiently change traffic signals. For a lot of the traffic lights in the United States, there are set amount of times for light switches. Our plan to relieve congestion in local street inspection is to decrease the time between light switches dependent upon the number of vehicles that accrue over a set amount of time. Developed in C++.

Performing First Aid

Performing First Aid is a mobile application designed to help inform users about the proper procedure to apply First Aid. The app lets users make decisions on what they think is the most appropriate decision and learn from them. Developed in Java.
